Edit Your Press Release











About:

I will take your draft release and turn it into an effective and precise document that will grab the attention of journalists. This means I'll help generate a catchy headline, if necessary, and reorganize and rewrite your content so that it reads more like a news article. This helps journalists envision your release as a story, one that they want to pitch or write. If your press release should really be a media alert, I will rewrite it in that format for you.
