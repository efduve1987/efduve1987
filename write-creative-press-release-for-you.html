Write A Creative Press Release For You











About:

As I have 5+ years experience working in a fast paced marketing agency, writing feature blogs and articles about multiple industries, I will be able to creatively write a press release for you, tailored to any industry in question. I have worked with high and reputable magazines in the KBB industry in particular, such as Country Life, KBB Review and EKBB Magazine to name but a few.



I also own my own Music PR company and create several press releases on a weekly basis, dealing with clients from around the world and with varying musical backgrounds and genres. I can write for B2B and B2C audiences, in both the trade and consumer sectors. Please get in touch and we can work out your requirements and the first steps on creating an effective press release for you!
